Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj

Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj (1630–1680) was a legendary Maratha king and a brilliant military strategist who founded the Maratha Empire in western India. He is celebrated for his courage, governance, naval innovations, and commitment to Swarajya (self-rule). His vision and values inspired generations and his leadership continues to be revered across India.

Why I Admire Him

  • He promoted the concept of Swarajya — self-rule and independence.
  • He respected all religions and emphasized justice and good governance.
  • He was a master of guerrilla warfare and built a strong, agile army.
